You end up playing the same three albums. Not because there's nothing new. Because nothing is trying to understand you.

What You Tell Aria Stays with Aria.

Your conversations are processed in the moment and never stored. Aria remembers your taste, not your words. Lock her with Face ID. We never know what you're listening to, who you are, or how you felt today.

No algorithmic feed. No streaks. No notifications pulling you back. Your feelings are not content.

Named After Someone Real.

Aria was built with the care you'd put into something you're naming after your daughter. One person, no investors, no engagement team — just the belief that music is too personal to be treated like content.

She exists because one person needed her before anyone else did.
